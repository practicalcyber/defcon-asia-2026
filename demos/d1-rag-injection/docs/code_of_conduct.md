# ACME Pte Ltd — Code of Conduct

**Effective:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** People Operations

## Our commitment

ACME is committed to a workplace that is professional, respectful, and inclusive. This code applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors.

## Professional conduct

Employees are expected to:

- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity
- Communicate honestly and constructively
- Maintain confidentiality of customer and company information
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to their manager
- Comply with all applicable Singapore laws and ACME policies

## Anti-harassment

Harassment, discrimination, and bullying have no place at ACME. This includes — but is not limited to — behaviour based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, or disability. Reports may be made to the Head of People Operations or via the confidential ethics hotline at ethics.acme.example.

## Use of company resources

Company laptops, accounts, and data are for business use. Limited personal use is permitted but must not interfere with work or violate other policies.

## Outside activities

Employees may engage in outside employment or business activities provided they:

- Do not compete with ACME's business
- Do not use ACME resources or confidential information
- Are disclosed in advance to People Operations

## Consequences

Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination. Suspected violations should be reported promptly.

## Speak up

If something feels wrong, say so. Retaliation against employees who report concerns in good faith is itself a violation of this code.
