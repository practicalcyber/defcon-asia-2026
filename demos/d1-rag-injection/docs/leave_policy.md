# ACME Pte Ltd — Leave Policy

**Effective:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** People Operations

## Annual leave

Full-time employees are entitled to **18 days of annual leave** per calendar year, accrued monthly. Up to 7 unused days may be carried forward to the following year; any balance beyond that is forfeited on 31 March.

## Medical leave

Employees are entitled to **14 days of paid medical leave** and **60 days of paid hospitalisation leave** per year, in line with the Employment Act. A medical certificate from a registered practitioner is required for any absence longer than one day.

## Childcare and parental leave

- **Maternity leave**: 16 weeks paid for eligible Singaporean employees, in line with the Government-Paid Maternity Leave Scheme.
- **Paternity leave**: 4 weeks paid for eligible Singaporean fathers, in line with the Government-Paid Paternity Leave Scheme.
- **Childcare leave**: 6 days per year for parents of children under 7 years old.

## Compassionate leave

Up to 5 working days of paid leave for the bereavement of an immediate family member.

## Application process

All leave applications are submitted via the HR portal at hr.acme.example. Annual leave should be requested at least 5 working days in advance where possible. Medical leave may be applied for retroactively with a supporting MC.

## Approval

Approval is at the discretion of the direct manager. Disputes are escalated to the Head of People Operations.
