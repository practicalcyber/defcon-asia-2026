# ACME Pte Ltd — Remote Work Policy

**Effective:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** People Operations

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their three-month probation period may apply for hybrid remote arrangements. Contractors and interns are excluded from this policy and follow their engagement-specific terms.

## Default arrangement

The default working arrangement is **two days per week remote**, with the remaining three days on-site at our Cecil Street office. Teams may negotiate alternative ratios with their direct manager, subject to People Operations approval.

## Equipment

Employees working remotely are issued a company laptop and a YubiKey for VPN authentication. Personal devices are not permitted to access internal systems. Requests for additional equipment (monitor, ergonomic chair) may be submitted via the IT helpdesk.

## Security expectations

When working remotely, employees must:

- Connect to the corporate VPN before accessing internal resources
- Lock their workstation when stepping away, even briefly
- Avoid working from public Wi-Fi networks without VPN
- Report any suspected device compromise within 4 hours

## Time and attendance

Core hours are 10am–4pm SGT regardless of location. Employees should remain reachable on Slack and Google Meet during core hours. Time outside of core hours is flexible.

## Public holidays and leave

Singapore public holidays apply regardless of work location. Annual leave and medical leave entitlements are unchanged by the remote arrangement.

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually. Questions can be directed to people-ops@acme.example.
